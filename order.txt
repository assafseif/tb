====================================================================
  NOTES
====================================================================
- MARKET and LIMIT orders  →  /fapi/v1/order  (standard endpoint)
- STOP_MARKET / TAKE_PROFIT_MARKET  →  /fapi/v1/algoOrder  (algo endpoint)
  - algoType=CONDITIONAL  (required)
  - triggerPrice=<price>  (not stopPrice)
  - closePosition=true    (closes full position, no qty needed)
- For a SELL position: flip side=BUY on all 3 steps
- triggerPrice on SL must be BELOW current price for a BUY position
- triggerPrice on TP must be ABOVE current price for a BUY position
